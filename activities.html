Departments

Community Service

Apart from the routine academic activities, College has been engaged in the developmental activities related to the rural and urban social structure. We conduct regular skill development programmes for the rural youth under the name CDTP (Community Development through Polytechnics) under the patronage of the Ministry of Skill Developement and Entrepreneurship (MSDE) of centrall Government. CDTP offers free vocational training and imparts technical skill to the rural youth and makes them confident in taking up a career related to their skill and ability. Self-employment rate has seen growing among such beneficiaries. They take up challenges in the field they are trained and become a part of nation building.

Continuing Education Cell

The college has established a subcentre of State Continuing Education Cell, which provides facility to undergo technical course in this institution for those who could not undergo formal education. These courses have enhanced the employability skills of rural youth. All theses programmes aim at technology dissemination to rural areas.

National Service Scheme(NSS)

The college has a National Service Scheme, which conducts various social services in the nearby area. It helps develop a social responsibility in every student and become a better human being. Our NSS unit has won national accolade many a times for their dedicated service to the society.

National Cadet Corps (NCC)

The NCC unit delivers remarkable service in the college under NCC 27 Bn. In the activities of national integration and dedication towards nation building, NCC is live and active through their camps, social service, volunteering and regular parades.

Bhoomithra Sena

Apart from their regular academic exercises, the students are engaged in the agricultural and environmental related activities, the students are involved in social missions through their “Bhoomithra Sena”. Social forestry, agriculture and other ecology activities are their main focus.

Alumni Association

The College is proud about its alumni which spread out through the entire globe. In every industry, one can find our alumni. Most of them are working in their respective engineering field, while some has been successful in building up of their own industry and some others find their own career of their interest. They are all settled in a comfortable way and are found to be grateful towards the college, for making them what they are today. The College has an Alumni Association, which is active in its functions like conducting get-togethers of alumni, extending helping hands to the needy students, contributing latest technology equipments to the College, helping other alumni in building up a successful career.

Parent-Teacher Association (PTA)

Without the support of the society, a college can’t fulfill its ultimate aim. The Parents of are involved in creating a constructive and conducive environment in the campus through a body like Parent Teacher Association. In all the issues related to academic and other activities, the PTA will have a say or a helping hand to extend. An effective PTA, indeed is proved to be an asset to our College.

Students Union:

To be familiar with the democratic way of functioning of a system, students engage in participatory democracy through their students union. Student representatives are elected by the students and forms the students union. They lead the student community in their arts, sports and other extra curricular activities.

Placement Cell:

The college has a placement cell working hard to help the students to find a job. The college has tie-up with some companies and they rely upon us for their technical man power requirement. HCL, L&T, Alstom, Eicher, V-guard Industries, MRF, Cadence, IBM, Arbee Constructions are some of the industries which have conducted placement drive in our college recently.

Entrepreneurship Developemnt

The college has innovative entrepreneurship developement council to promote the entrepreneurial skill of students. It functions under the guidance of Kerala Start-up mission. They conduct entrepreneurship training sessions, exhibitions etc.

Industry Interaction

The college has signed MoU with Palghat Management Association(PMA), which is a body comprising of the representatives of Management of all the industries in the district. PMA is keen in co-operating with us for each of our industry interactive programmes.
